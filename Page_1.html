THE KETOGENIC DIET

Today, there is so much talk about the ketogenic diet.
You may wonder what the Keto diet is all about?
It is a low carbohydrate, high fat diet.
Keto dieters eat a minimum of 50grams of carbohydrate a day, and maintain a strick count of calorie intake.
70% of their diet is fat based, and the remaining 30% is shared between protein and vegetables.
