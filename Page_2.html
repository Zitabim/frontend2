How does it work?

Our bodies are attuned to use Carbohydrates for energy and any excess is stored as fat in cells for future use.
Cutting back on carbohydrates, causes our bodies to use up the stored fat for energy, leading to weight loss.
At this stage, your body is said to be in Ketogenesis.
Apart from weight loss, the Ketogenic diet has been proven to control HBA1c for type 2 diabetics, when done along with exercise.
