Physical touristic Potentials

Cameroon is blessed with a multitude of touristic sites.
These include the sandy beaches of Limbe and Kribi, fast flowing rivers like the Sanaga, Wouri and Mezam,
waterfalls and rapids like the Ekom Kam, Menchum and Abi falls and the Bamenda high plateau with its rocky outcrops.
These sites are easily accesible by all season roads.
