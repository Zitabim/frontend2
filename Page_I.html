TRADITIONAL ATTIRE

The "Atogho" is the pride of every North Westener.
It has evolved to incorporate modern fashion trends.
Initially, it comprised of colourful thread markings on a black background.
These markings vary depending on social status and traditional hierachy.
Today, other background colors are used, including, Red, Blue and white.
The "Atogho" is worn during important ceremonies like Marriages and Death Celebrations. Find below a couple dressed in the "Atogho".
